Hyperdemon

I've been playing a shitload of Hyperdemon lately. It's such a cool game, move with style, kill demons, and grind until you rank up in the ladder. It's the sequel to Sorath's studio Devil Daggers, but the scoring works different: instead of surviving for as long as you can, in this game you need to kill as much shit as you can, and as fast as you can.

Check it out on Steam!
